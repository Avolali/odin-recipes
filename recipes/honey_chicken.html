Honey Glazed Chicken

Description

This is the recipe for "Honey Glazed Chicken" which is a delicious light meal that everyone will enjoy. It is best served hot with steamed rice.

Feel free to follow along this recipe or alter it to your taste. The prep time is about 10 minutes and the cook time is also 10 minutes so it should take around 20 minutes in total!

Ingredients

  • ¼ cup honey
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • ⅛ teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 1½ tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves, cut into bite-size pieces

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Whisk honey, soy sauce and rep pepper flakes in a bowl; set aside.
  3. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat; cook and stir chicken in hot oil until lightly brown, about 5 minutes.
  4. Pour honey mixture into the skillet; continue to cook and stir until chicken is no longer pink in the center and sauce is thickened, about 5 minutes more
  5. Serve hot and enjoy!
